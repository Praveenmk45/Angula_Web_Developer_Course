The Best Movies According to Praveen

My Top 5 movies of all-time.

Geetha Govindam

A simple, innocent lecturer is misunderstood by a woman who despises him, but as they are forced to spend time together, misunderstandings are cleared and love blossoms between them.

Jannu

"Jaanu" is a Telugu romantic drama about two childhood sweethearts, Ram and Jaanu, who reunite at their school reunion after many years. Despite their deep love for each other, circumstances kept them apart, and their reunion brings back memories, emotions, and a bittersweet closure to their unfulfilled romance.

Sita Ramam

Lieutenant Ram, an orphaned army officer at the Kashmir border, receives anonymous letters from a woman named Sita Mahalakshmi. Determined to meet her, Ram's search turns into a touching romance complicated by secrets and sacrifice. Their love story unfolds across time, leading to unconditional loyalty and ultimate closure years later through the eyes of another character, Afreen.

Amaran

"Amaran" is a biographical drama based on the life of Major Mukund Varadarajan, an Indian Army officer who was posthumously awarded the Ashoka Chakra. The story is told through the eyes of his wife, Indhu, as she reminisces about their journeyâ€”from meeting in college, falling in love despite religious and familial obstacles, to their marriage and family life. Mukund's deep commitment leads him to serve in Kashmir, where he bravely fights against militants and forms close bonds with his fellow soldiers. Ultimately, Mukund sacrifices his life during a difficult counter-terrorist operation, leaving behind a grieving but proud family. The film highlights the emotional struggles and valor of soldiers and their families, ending with Indhu accepting the Ashoka Chakra in honor of her late husband.

Dear Comrade

"Dear Comrade" is a romantic drama about Bobby, a fiery student union leader with anger issues, who falls in love with Lily, a talented state-level cricketer. However, Bobby's inability to control his temper threatens their relationship, leading to heartbreak and a journey of self-realization as he strives to win back Lily and support her ambitions.
